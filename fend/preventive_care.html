Causes of Diabetes

Diabetes is caused by a variety of factors including genetics, lifestyle choices, and environmental factors. Some common causes are:

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Poor diet high in sugar and fat
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Obesity
  • Age

How to Prevent Diabetes

Preventing diabetes involves making healthy lifestyle choices:

  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Avoid smoking
  • Limit alcohol intake
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Regular medical check-ups

Different Kinds of Diabetes

There are three main types of diabetes:

  • Type 1 Diabetes
  • Type 2 Diabetes
  • Gestational Diabetes

Home Remedies for Diabetes

Some home remedies can help manage diabetes:

  • Consuming bitter gourd juice
  • Drinking fenugreek water
  • Including cinnamon in diet
  • Consuming aloe vera gel

Statistics

Diabetes affects millions of people worldwide. Some key statistics are:

  • Approximately 422 million people have diabetes globally.
  • In the US, 34.2 million people have diabetes.
  • 1.6 million deaths are directly attributed to diabetes each year.

What to Avoid

People with diabetes should avoid:

  • Sugary foods and beverages
  • Refined carbohydrates
  • Trans fats
  • Excessive alcohol
  • Smoking

Food Items That Help Reduce Diabetes

Some foods can help manage blood sugar levels:

  • Leafy greens
  • Whole grains
  • Fatty fish
  • Beans and lentils
  • Berries

Specialists for Diabetes

Specialists who treat diabetes include:

  • Endocrinologists
  • Diabetologists
  • Nutritionists
  • Primary care physicians

Symptoms of Diabetes

Common symptoms of diabetes include:

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
